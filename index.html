I am a postdoctoral researcher at MIT and Biohub, advised by Sergey Ovchinnikov and Aly A Khan.

I recently finished my PhD at The University of Texas at Austin, advised by Philipp Krähenbühl and Adam Klivans. I was previously a research assistant at UC Berkeley, working with Professors Jitendra Malik and Amir Zamir. I graduated from UC Berkeley with a Bachelors of Science degree.

I am broadly interested in applying machine learning to biological applications.
